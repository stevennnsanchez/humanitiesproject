Map of the Spanish Empire during the Golden Age

During the Spanish Golden Age, art, literature, and political power moved together across a vast imperial network. This map shows the transregional circulation connecting Mexico City to Spain and Asia, the same system within which La Respuesta was written and read. That context matters for our argument: Sor Juana was not sending a private letter into a closed room. She was writing inside a world where texts traveled, and she knew it.
